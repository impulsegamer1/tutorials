How to make stone

Step one get a furnace, Step two get wood to put in furnace, Step three put the wood on the bottom square, Step four get cobblestone, step five put cobblestone in the top square, step six let the cobblestone smelt and you get stone

How to make a crafting table

step one, you first get wood from a tree, step two, you go into you inventory by pressing E, step three, place wood inside the 2x2 grid, step four, take wooden planks from the grid, step five, fill the grid with wooden planks, step six, take out the crafting table.
